# UX Copy

Use to write or fix interface text: labels, buttons, empty/error states, onboarding, tooltips.

## Principles
- **Clear over clever.** Say what happens; lead with the user's goal.
- **Consistent voice & terms.** One term per concept; match the product glossary/brand.
- **Action-oriented buttons** — "Save changes", not "OK"/"Submit".
- **Helpful errors** — what went wrong *and* how to fix it; no blame, no codes-only.
- **Empty states teach** the next action. Onboarding is short and skippable.
- **Concise** — cut filler, front-load meaning, sentence case unless the brand says otherwise.

## Procedure
1. Identify each string's job and the user's state. 2. Draft in the product voice. 3. Tighten.
4. Check consistency across the whole flow (terms, casing, tone).

## Done when
Every string is clear, consistent, action-oriented, and matches the product voice.
